A Knight's Sonnet

Taken from Erhetd'n Naex

8024.096.6

Inside this cave, a chill
Pervades the space between
Imposing stone and still
Nocturnal air; serene
Echoic semi-silence
Filters through the chambers,
Only finding balance,
Resonance, reminders.
Then I gasp again,
Hold back no more the breath
Entrancing me, regain
Emotion, rise from death.
Can't stop it, can't rescind;
The cavern howls with wind.
